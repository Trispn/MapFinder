How MapFinder Works

Discover nearby places effortlessly with MapFinder. Here's how it works:

Step 1: Search for a Place

Type in the name of a location or use your current location to start exploring nearby places.

Step 2: Apply Filters

Refine your search using advanced filters like price range, rating, distance, cuisine type, and more.

Step 3: Explore Results

View detailed information about each place, including ratings, reviews, photos, and contact details.

Step 4: Get Directions

Click on "Get Directions" to navigate to your chosen destination using Google Maps.

Step 5: View Street View

Enable Street View to explore the area around your selected location in immersive detail.
